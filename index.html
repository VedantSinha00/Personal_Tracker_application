How this tracker works
Minimum viable day MVD
The smallest version of a productive day that still keeps momentum alive. Not failure — choosing to protect the thread rather than break it entirely.

1. Attend classes
2. One 25-minute work block on anything
3. One real rest interval — music or lying down, no screen
Mark with the MVD button on each day card.
Full rest day Rest
When your body or mind genuinely could not show up — not even for MVD. Not laziness. Your nervous system occasionally needs a complete stop.

One per week is fine. Multiple is a signal — usually something external is draining you.

Make rest actually restful: music without screens, lying down, being outside. Not a show.
Mark with Full rest. Disables MVD for that day.
The stack
The decision you make on Sunday so you don't have to make it mid-week. Write the single next concrete action per area.

When you sit down to work, you're not deciding what to do — you're just executing what you already decided. This removes the friction that makes you open a show instead.

Drag items to reorder priority within High or Low focus.
Update every Sunday in the Stack tab.
Real rest vs. shows
You watch shows actively — tracking characters, figuring things out. That's entertainment, not rest. It doesn't restore you.

Real rest quietens input rather than replacing it: music alone, lying down without a screen, being outside. At least one daily, ideally right after classes as a transition ritual.
Track with the Rest checkbox. Shows belong on weekends.
